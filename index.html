Frequently Asked Questions

How is the fairness score calculated?
Our algorithm compares player values using current season stats, positional rankings, and recent performance trends. The score shows the point difference between the two teams.

How do I enter player names?
Use full player names for best results. You can also include position abbreviations if needed (e.g., "Patrick Mahomes QB"). Simply type or paste names into the input boxes in Manual Mode, or use the position cards in Assisted Mode.
What does the Reset button do?
The Reset button clears all entered player names from both teams. A warning will appear before clearing to prevent accidental data loss.
Can I analyze multiple trades?
Yes! After viewing results, click "Analyze New Trade" to modify your player lists and run another comparison without losing your previous entries.
